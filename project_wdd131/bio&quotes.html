Inception

    **Biography:** Arthur is Cobb's reliable and meticulously organized "point man."     **Biography:** Ariadne is a brilliant architecture student recruited by Cobb to design the intricate and paradoxical dreamscapes for the inception mission.     **Biography:** Mr. Saito is a powerful and enigmatic Japanese businessman who initially tries to extract information from Cobb but then offers him the inception job.     **Biography:** Yusuf is the team's skilled chemist, responsible for formulating the powerful sedatives necessary for the deep, multi-layered dreams required for inception.   • Cillian Murphy
    Role: Robert Fischer
    Info: The heir whose mind is targeted for inception.
    **Biography:** Robert Fischer is the heir to a vast corporate empire and the target of Cobb's inception mission.     Role: Browning
    Info: Fischer's godfather and business associate.

  • Marion Cotillard

    Info: Cobb's deceased wife, who haunts his dreams.
    **Biography:** Mal is Dom Cobb's deceased wife, who tragically died by suicide, leaving Cobb under suspicion for her murder. She appears as a powerful and destructive projection within Cobb's subconscious, constantly trying to sabotage his missions and pull him back into a shared dream limbo. Her presence is the source of much of Cobb's guilt and internal conflict.

  • Michael Caine
    Role: Miles

    **Biography:** Professor Stephen Miles is Cobb's father-in-law and mentor, and a university professor who teaches dream architecture.     **Quotes:** "Come back to reality, Dom."
