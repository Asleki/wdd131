Meet the Developer

I built CineWatch to solve the everyday problem of scattered entertainment content. With my background in full-stack web development and user experience design, I envisioned a platform that’s visually clean, fast, and curated for real film lovers — whether casual watchers or hardcore binge warriors.

Beyond coding, I enjoy movies that make you think, animations that surprise you, and exploring web innovations that connect people with what they love.
