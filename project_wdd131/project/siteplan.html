CineWatch - Website Planning Document

A blueprint for designing and developing the CineWatch project.

1. Site Name

Site Name: CineWatch

Description: I chose "CineWatch" as the site name because it's a concise and clear combination of "Cinema" (referring to movies and shows) and "Watch" (indicating the primary user action). It's memorable, easy to pronounce, and directly communicates the site's purpose: a platform for watching and discovering cinematic content. The name aims for a modern and accessible feel, suitable for a broad audience interested in entertainment.

Optional Domain Availability: cinewatch.com (Conceptual, not verified for actual availability)

2. Site Purpose

The primary purpose of the CineWatch website is to serve as a comprehensive hub for discovering and exploring TV shows and movies. It aims to provide users with detailed information about various titles, including release dates, IMDb ratings, genres, creators, cast, famous quotes, and a synopsis. Additionally, it highlights where content can be watched (e.g., Netflix, Showmax) and indicates if a subscription is required. The site will feature trending and international shows, making it easy for users to find new content and revisit classics.

3. Scenarios

Here are two scenarios reflecting potential target audience users of CineWatch:

  1. Scenario 1: "I just finished watching 'Breaking Bad' and loved the quotes. I wonder if this site has a section where I can easily find famous quotes from my favorite shows, and maybe even discover similar shows to watch next?"
  2. Scenario 2: "I'm looking for a new action-thriller series to binge-watch, but I'm not sure what's popular or highly-rated right now. Can CineWatch help me discover trending shows with good IMDb scores and tell me where I can stream them?"

4. Color Schema

My color schema is designed to be clean, modern, and user-friendly, with a focus on readability and visual appeal in both light and dark modes.

Light Mode Palette:

  • #dbe5f5 - Background Color (Light Mode): Used for the main body background, providing a soft, light blue-gray base.
  • #333333 - Primary Text Color (Light Mode): Used for general body text, headings, and most informative text, ensuring high readability on light backgrounds.
  • #007bff - Accent/Primary Action Color: Used for links, buttons (e.g., "Watch Now"), active pagination dots, and border accents (e.g., quote left border).
  • #c5e0f3 - Card Background Color (Light Mode): Used for show cards and other prominent content blocks, providing a slightly darker shade of blue-gray than the main background.
  • #ffffff - Quote Container/Sub-Card Background (Light Mode): Used for individual quote containers and potentially other nested content, offering a crisp white.
  • #e0e0e0 - Sidebar Background (Light Mode): Used for the Famous Quotes and Overview sidebars, a distinct light gray.

Dark Mode Palette:

  • #1a1a1a - Background Color (Dark Mode): The main body background for a deep, dark theme.
  • #f0f0f0 - Primary Text Color (Dark Mode): Ensures high contrast and readability on dark backgrounds.
  • #8ab4f8 - Accent/Primary Action Color (Dark Mode): A brighter blue for links, buttons, and accents, ensuring visibility in the dark theme.
  • #2a2a2a - Card Background Color (Dark Mode): Darker background for show cards and prominent content blocks.
  • #333333 - Quote Container/Sub-Card Background (Dark Mode): Slightly lighter than the main dark background for nested content.
  • #262626 - Sidebar Background (Dark Mode): A distinct dark gray for sidebars.

5. Typography

I will be using primarily one font family to maintain consistency and optimize loading times, while utilizing different weights and styles for hierarchy.

  • Font Family: Arial, sans-serif
  • Application:
    • Headings (e.g., H1, H2, H3): Arial, Bold
    • Body Text (Paragraphs, List Items): Arial, Regular
    • Quotes and Small Text: Arial, Italic (for quotes), Arial, Regular (for captions/small text)

The choice of Arial, sans-serif ensures wide browser compatibility and excellent readability across various devices. Its clean, neutral aesthetic aligns well with a content-focused platform like CineWatch.
